Our program

Our 8-month program is divided into 12 modules. The last module is a 6-weeks intensive Final Project where students work on a real project with a real partner. Our training takes place at BeCentral (Brussels) every Sunday. During the week, students are supported online in their self-study, homework and peer learning.

Learn more!

Our project

Hack Your Future is an initiative that wants to help refugees and newcomers integrate and become and active part of society by facilitating access to labour market in the technology industry. In Belgium, HYF has successfully taught several classes and it's very excited to have it's first version in Antwerp.
